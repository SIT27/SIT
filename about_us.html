About us

SIT is a fresh take on dog training. We're a passionate team of four on a mission to bring dog training to the people — and show that a new beginning with your trusty companion is easier than you think.

SIT fits into your life. With packed schedules and pups who just need that little nudge in the right direction, SIT is the flexible, friendly training course you’ve been looking for.

Our vision

At SIT, we imagine a world where the bond between humans and dogs is built on emotional connection. This is the foundation of all learning. Our mission is to create a space where every dog owner feels empowered to truly listen, understand, and connect with their four-legged friend.

We believe that only through understand your dog you can guide them towards better behavior, forging relationships that are not only obedient, but deeply harmonious. This is more than training—it’s a fresh start where trust, respect, and emotional intelligence lead the way, creating connections that go beyond commands and touch the heart.
