About us

SIT is a student-made brand that connects dogowners through dogtraining

Together, as a group of four we're passionate to bring dog training to the people, and prove that a fresh start with your trusty compagnion is easy.

SIT will adjust to your schedule.

With busy schedules and dogs that need just that little nudge in the right direction, SIT can be the training course you need!

Our vision

At SIT, we imagine a world where the bond between humans and dogs is built on emotional connection. This is the foundation of all learning. Our mission is to create a space where every dog owner feels empowered to truly listen, understand, and connect with their four-legged friend. We believe that only through understand your dog you can guide them towards better behavior, forging relationships that are not only obedient, but deeply harmonious. This is more than training—it’s a fresh start where trust, respect, and emotional intelligence lead the way, creating connections that go beyond commands and touch the heart.
